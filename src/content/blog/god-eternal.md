---
title: 'God Eternal'
description: 'Whatever changes there is one constant'
pubDate: 'Mar 27 2026'
---
I've seen the whispering sands of time\
Scattered on desert dunes\
Flowing as it glitters and shines\
Cresting troughs in windswept orange hues

And I've seen the turbulent water of life\
Falling from the sky and little drops of dew\
Coursing the earth, becoming the tide\
Gathering in ocean's turquoise blue

And I wonder what am I, in this endless age\
Mere man, fragile, finite and new\
Looking at this world like I'm on a stage\
Dwarfed by the infinite, unending view

But as I gaze at the heavens so bright\
Stunned by the majestic, boundless sight\
I know the God who created this dome\
Will one day call me to Him, my home
