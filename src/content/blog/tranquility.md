---
title: 'Tranquility'
description: 'Hope for a better tomorrow'
pubDate: 'Mar 24 2026'
---
The sea still foams on the golden shore\
Waves lap the crystal sand\
And still stand the mountains of yore\
Towering over mythical land

The stars still twinkle from far, on high\
And the moon still shows her smile\
Against the deep, dark carpet of the sky\
All who see, to beguile

The sun shines and dawn still breaks\
Morning dew settles on meadows green\
Birds still sing and glide over shiny lakes\
Animals still their offspring wean

When all the pain and anger settles like a haze\
And strife and sorrow cease\
There will be a morrow when the golden rays\
Splay across a world at peace
